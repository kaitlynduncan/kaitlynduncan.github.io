Interests & Hobbies

Baking

I love to bake because it helps me destress and ground myself. The best part about having baking as a hobby is getting to share baked goods with my loved ones. Some of my favorite things to bake are: maple snickerdoodles, red velvet cupcakes, and cheese danishes.

Travel

I've loved travel since I was a kid. Living outside of the US gave my family the ability to travel so many places when I was younger. I hope to keep to keep exploring new places as I move into a new chapter of my life. Some of my favorite places I've been are: Rome, Athens, Amsterdam, Siem Reap, and Boracay.

Reading

Reading is my favorite pastime. I've read so many books that I've lost count. My current read is We Are Dreams in the Eternal Machine by Deni Ellis BÃ©chard.

Gaming

My favorite games to play right now are the Survivor trilogy Tomb Raider games. I also really enjoy playing Stardew Valley, various board games, and chess.

Ice Skating

I took ice skating lessons when I was in elementary school, but didn't keep up with it as I grew up. Recently, I've been making an effort to get back out on the ice and redevelop some of the skills I lost. Skating is a great way to clear your mind and get some exercise.
