Kaitlyn Duncan

Senior Computer Science and Film Studies student at the University of San Francisco.

I am currently a Senior at the University of San Francisco. I'm pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with a minor in Film Studies. Outside of class I am involved in community organizations like KUSF (our college radio station), USFtv, and Qmmunity. I've also participated in events like the Women in Tech Donshack. I will be graduating Summa Cum Laude in May 2026 and I'm excited to get out there and get professional experience in the fields I'm passionate about.

Interests & Hobbies

Baking

I love to bake because it helps me destress and ground myself. The best part about having baking as a hobby is getting to share baked goods with my loved ones. Some of my favorite things to bake are: maple snickerdoodles, red velvet cupcakes, and cheese danishes.

I've loved travel since I was a kid. Living outside of the US gave my family the ability to travel so many places when I was younger. I hope to keep to keep exploring new places as I move into a new chapter of my life. Some of my favorite places I've been are: Rome, Athens, Amsterdam, Siem Reap, and Boracay.
